Our original adventure queen & her adventure machine, @anka_martin & her Juliana Maverick💚

Margaux Elliott rode her Maverick 6,292 miles over the course of about 1,000 hours and 253 days to accomplish her goal of climbing ONE MILLION feet in 2020. “Even though there was as much descending as climbing, I always referred to it as a challenge to climb one million feet because climbing was the thing I was always bad at. Focusing on something that had always been a weakness of mine and overcoming the challenge has been empowering and gave me confidence both on and off the bike.” You can read Margaux’s whole interview on Pinkbike with the link in our bio. Illustrations: @pinkbike / #gojuliana #gomargaux

More big news coming down the pike - we are thrilled to welcome elite cross country racers Rose Grant (@rosekgrant) & Evelyn Dong (@evelynd11) to the Juliana family 🎉 Rose & Evelyn bring with them an impressive resume of results in both the cross country & gravel disciplines including winning Leadville 100 & Crusher in the Tushar, multiple national championship top 3 results, and multiple World Cup & World Championships starts. Rose & Evelyn will be lining up for numerous Epic Rides & gravel events throughout the 2021 season and we can’t wait to see what these women can accomplish under the Juliana flag. Hit the link in our bio to learn more about these two superstars. 🌟

We’d like to give a great big welcome to a woman who hardly needs an introduction, @badgal_brooky 💙 Brooklyn is not only out to push herself on the bike, but to push the boundaries of the sport to be more inclusive & diverse. “I hope to be someone who sets a good example and shows that biking, being a part of the sport or even progression doesn’t have to look a certain way.” We think you’re doing a damn good job so far, Brooklyn. Get to know a bit more about Brooklyn with the link in our bio. ✌🏾
